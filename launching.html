Launching into Cybersecurity

The first module of the MSc Cybersecurity.

An introduction to cybersecurity!

Follow this page for regular updates in my study for this module. This module will be looking at the bigger picture of Cyber Security. What is it? Why is it such major issue for organisations? And finally, what role do Cyber Security professionals play in organisations?
